The Xbox Platform

Discussion

Bink now runs on Microsoft's Xbox game console! The Xbox is fun to work with - all the good things about Windows with almost all of the annoying things removed. We're huge fans of the Xbox here at RAD!

With the new register combiner display method, you can use Bink on Xbox to play HDTV (1280x720) videos! And conveniently, if you aren't on a HDTV display, you can just stretch the movie down to the lower resolution.

Bink is extremely easy to use and integrate under this platform. This chapter will discuss everything you need to know!
