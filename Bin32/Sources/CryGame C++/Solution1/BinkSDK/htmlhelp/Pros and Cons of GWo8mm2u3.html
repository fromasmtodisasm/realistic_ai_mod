Pros and Cons of GWorld Blitting under MacOS

Discussion

GWorld blitting is good, because:

1) It is the most compatible way of getting pixels onto the screen. They will always work.

2) GWorlds can perform clipping during blitting.

3) GWorlds can be scaled to any resolution - not just 2x (this is usually slow, though).


GWorld blitting is bad, because:

1) GWorlds are slow! Even if CopyBits was perfect (which it ain't), there still is an extra frame buffer blit compared to direct-to-screen.

3) GWorlds use more memory. Again, because there is an extra frame buffer, they require more memory than direct-to-screen.
