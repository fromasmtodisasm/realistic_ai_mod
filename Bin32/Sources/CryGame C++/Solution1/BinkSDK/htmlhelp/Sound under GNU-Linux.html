Sound under GNU/Linux

Discussion

For sound support under GNU/Linux, Bink supplies built support for SDL_Mixer, the common library to play multiple audio tracks under GNU/Linux. To use this support, you must use the BinkSoundUseSDLMixer in your application. If you don't use the SDL mixer, then you can also tie into Bink's audio support directly - email us for more details.
