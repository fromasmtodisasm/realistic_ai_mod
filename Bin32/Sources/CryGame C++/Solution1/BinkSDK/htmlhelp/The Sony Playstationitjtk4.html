The Sony Playstation 2 Platform

Discussion

Bink now runs on Sony's complicated but powerful PS2 console. The PS2 is crazy complicated and surprisingly old school. It is really hard to get good performance out of this box - but you know what? We love it! We get closer to maximizing the absolute best performance out of this platform than probably any other. But then we're pretty old school ourselves, so there you go.

In any case, after a lot of work, Bink is extremely easy to use and integrate under this platform. This chapter will discuss everything you need to know!
